A wide range of features is included in the residences at Sobha Vista to ensure a comfortable living environment. With their spacious design, the new towers provide a panoramic view of the sprawling urban park. An opulent lifestyle that is truly true to Lutyens' architectural ethos is offered by Sobha City Lutyens' Lifestyle. In Tower Z there are three homes per floor and in Tower D there are double-height balconies on each floor. Also, there is a dedicated drop-off for each tower. Sobha Vista Residences New Launch comprises of 6 towers with 396 units. A total of 8.9 acres are dedicated to open and green space, with over 4000 trees and one acre of waterbodies that contribute to the preservation of a temperate climate.
